## Data Cleaning Steps
- Removed duplicate records
- Filled missing values in "amount" using median
- Replaced missing "country" with "Unknown"
- Converted "order_date" to datetime format

## Analysis Performed
- Total revenue calculation
- Revenue by country
- Revenue by order status
- Monthly revenue trends
- Customer revenue contribution

## Key Insights
- Portugal generates the highest revenue
- Completed orders drive most of the revenue
- Cancelled orders negatively impact performance
- A small group of customers contributes a large share of revenue
